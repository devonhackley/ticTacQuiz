Rules

Users can either play using our Default quiz, or make up their own questions to create their own quiz!

    How To Play:

  1. Starting a new game:
    • From the Home screen, click on "Let's Play".
    • Enter each player name - make sure they don't match!
    • Select the quiz you want to play. You can use our default quiz, or create and try your own!
  2. Game Flow:
    • Player 1 goes first. On your turn, click on any square with a "?" marker. A question box will appear - answer the question correctly to take and mark that square!
    • Answer wrong, and you will get NOTHING. Either way, it's now your opponent's turn.
    • Players alternate turns until either player WINS by getting 3 squares in a row, or until the whole grid is filled and there's no winner.
  3. Tiebreaker Questions:
    • If the same square is answered incorrectly twice, it will turn red.
    • Clicking a red square will bring up a tiebreaker question: answer it right and you get the square, but answer wrong and that square goes to your opponent! Approach with caution.
