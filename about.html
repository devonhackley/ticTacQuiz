Peter Tynan

My name is Peter. I'm 24 years old, born and raised here in Seattle, WA - Ballard to be exact! At this time 2 years ago, I was student teaching 6th grade math at Shuksan Middle School in Bellingham. I realized that wasn't the path I wanted to take, and so at this time 1 year ago I finished my Bachelor's degree in Math, enlisted in the Washington National Guard and left for training. I learned networking concepts through my IT Specialist role in the army, but software development was much more appealing to me and I jumped at the opportunity to be trained by AWS and CodeFellows. My experiences - both as a teacher and as a student - have helped me develop a strong understanding of myself as a learner, and how I can play to my strengths while working with my weaknesses. I am so excited to learn more software development; I think that automation will continue to be increasingly important in the future, and I want to be there as it develops. I hope I can develop automation that can be used ethically and to the benefit of all people, not only the privileged.
