Rules

Users can either play using our Default quiz, or make up their own questions to create their own quiz!

    How To Play:

  1. Click "Let's Play"
  2. Choose whether to be player 1 or 2 and select your choice of "X" or "O".
  3. Click "Start Game"
  4. Choose any of the squares.
  5. When the question box pops up, select the correct answer and hit submit.
  6. If you're answer is correct, you'll win the square. If you're answer is incorrect the other player will have a chance to "steal" that square. By answering a new question correcctly. If both players answer incorrectly then a tie breaker True or False question will be asked.
  7. The other player will have a chance to answer the True/False question first.
  8. Steals does NOT count as a turn.
